OUR PROJECT


The communities targeted to empower in this survey are UCLA students from any financial background, who have previously or currently held a job on campus in order to support themselves. This project is meant to shed light on this issue and make the UCLA community more aware of the problems caused by balancing a job and academics on working students.

More information about our surveyed students

Students here at UCLA get jobs for many different reasons, as evidenced by clicking through the interactive map. This website creates a place for student workers to share their stories and struggles, and show that if you are struggling with having a job, you are not alone. Many others face the same challenges as you, and there are resources to help you succeed! CAPS is an option for UCLA students.

Hello! We wanted to create a space for student workers at UCLA to share their stories and struggles when it comes to working while being a student. To read these students' stories, interact with the map by clicking on the chart, based on if you would like to view stories from students working on or off campus, and then based on why a student got a job.
